Jollof Rice

Ingredient Quantity
Rice 2 cups
Tomato Paste 1/2 cup
Chicken 500g

Steps:

  1. Heat oil and fry onions until golden.
  2. Add tomato paste and spices, simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Add rice and broth (water in which meat or vegetables has been boiled), cook for 20 minutes.

Egusi Soup

Ingredient Quantity
Ground Egusi (melon seeds) 1 cup
Beef or Goat Meat 500g
Palm Oil 1/4 cup
Ground Crayfish 2 tablespoons
Spinach or Bitterleaf 2 cups (chopped)

Steps:

  1. Boil the meat with onions, seasoning cubes, and salt until soft.
  2. Heat palm oil in a pot, then add ground egusi and stir continuously.
  3. Add meat stock to the egusi mixture and let it cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in the cooked meat, crayfish, and chopped vegetables.
  5. Allow the soup to bubble gently for an additional 10 minutes before serving.

Suya

Ingredient Quantity
Beef (thinly sliced) 500g
Yaji 3 tablespoons
Groundnut Oil 2 tablespoons
Salt For taste
Onions (sliced) 1 large
Tomatoes (sliced) 2 medium

Steps:

  1. Mix the suya spice, salt, and oil together.
  2. Coat the beef slices in the spice mixture and leave to soak for 1 hour.
  3. Rod the beef slices and grill over hot coals or in an oven at 180Â°C for 20-30 minutes.
  4. Turn the meat occasionally until fully cooked and browned.
  5. Serve with sliced onions and tomatoes.
